Early Life

Toph is from the Gaoling, Earth Kingdom. Her full name is Toph Beifong.Toph is the only child of the Beifong family. Born blind, she is raised by her parents and concealed from the world. Her parents believe that her blindness makes her incapable of taking care of herself. Nobody knew that Toph existed and she was expected to be well-mannered due to her noble status. As a result, Toph resents the way her parents raised her as a child. One day, when she was young Toph ran away from home and hid in a cave filled with badgermoles. She copied the badgermole’s movements through the vibrations she felt from the ground and through her sense of hearing, enabling her to learn earthbending. Her blindness allowed Toph to develop patience and keen hearing, thus allowing her to become a successful earthbender. Toph’s father hired an earthbending master, Master Yu, to instruct her. However, her father ordered Master Yu to teach her beginner moves. She secretly trained to become a master earthbender and fought in underground earthbending tournaments. She was under a disguise called the Blind Bandit and she has been Earth Rumble champion for many years. Her entire life changes when Aang finds in her in a Earth Rumble tournament.

Appearance

Toph has light skin, sea foam green eyes, and black hair. Toph wears a traditional upper class earth nation outfit consisting of a green dress with a yellow tunic. Her hair is worn in a bun and she wears a bun with a yellow and green striped headband, accessorized with two white pom poms on the side of her ears. She wears her hair with bangs. Toph is 12 years old.

Mission

After meeting Aang at an Earth Rumble tournament, Toph has found her escape route to leave home and help Aang learn Earthbending. She will teach Aang to be patient and listen in combat. She is very powerful and will do her job to protect Team Avatar and is willing to help Aang during his fight with Ozai.

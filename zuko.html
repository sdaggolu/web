Early Life

Zuko is the firstborn prince of Firelord Ozai and Princess Ursa. His paternal great-grandfather,the one who started the Hundred Years War, is Firelord Sozin, and his maternal great-grandfather is the avatar before Aang, Avatar Roku. From a young age, Zuko was disdained by his father. When Zuko was 11 years old, his uncle Iroh’s son was killed during the siege to conquer Ba Sing Se (the Earth Kingdom’s capital). Ozai used Iroh’s weakness and requested an audience with his father, Firelord Azulon, and asked to be heir to the throne instead of his older brother Iroh. Azulon angrily said to Ozai that he should know how it feels to lose a son. That night, Ursa went missing and Azulon’s father died, allowing Ozai to become the Firelord. Zuko was devastated that his mother was gone because she has always supported Zuko in all his endeavors. One day, when he was 13, Zuko sat in a conference room and spoke out when he wasn't supposed to, thus disrespecting his father. Zuko had to face an agni kai (a fire duel), but was unaware that his opponent was his father. During the match, Zuko refused to fight his father and Ozai punished Zuko for his cowardliness by burning Zuko’s face, leaving a scar on his left eye. Zuko was banished and was told that he could return to the throne and regain his honor by capturing the avatar, who was the last airbender.

Appearance

Zuko has light skin, hazel eyes, and black hair. Zuko wears a traditional fire army uniform consisting of red armor. This armor has shoulder plates and covers the neck, arm, and lower body. This armor is worn over a loose long-sleeved gray turtle neck shirt and pants. He wears brown arm warmers and a brown belt that is buckled with a golden tile. His hair is shaved except at the top, which is tied in a long ponytail using a red hairband. Zuko has a red scar on his left eye. Zuko is 16 years old.

Mission

Zuko has always had a mission to capture Aang in order to restore honor and regain his seat at the throne. However, as he travels around the world, searching for the avatar, Zuko has many impactful realizations under the guidance of his uncle Iroh. Zuko learns that Aang will give the world hope and he must help Aang learn firebending in order to defeat the Firelord and maintain peace in the world. Defeating the Firelord would also allow Zuko to become heir to the throne. Zuko aims to maintain peace and love throughout the world when he manages to become a Firelord.

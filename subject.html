Aang

Aang is the avatar, master of all four elements: air, water, earth, and fire. As an airbender, Aang has a blue arrow tattoo on his forehead. Aang was told that he was the avatar at the age of 12. When he found it, he was very anxious because he knew the fate of the world lies in his shoulders. One day, he overheard a conversation from the monks and they said that Aang had to leave his home to train somewhere else. Aang was really upset and ran away with his air bison named Appa. They reached the south pole during a rain storm and Aang was stuck in a block of ice. A hundred years later, he was discovered by Katara and Sokka and they are on a mission to defeat the firelord and save the world.

Katara

Toph

Toph is a blind earthbender who uses that weakness to her advantage by feeling the vibrations of the earth. She ran away from her parents because she wanted to teach Aang Earthbending. She was a pampered girl who was hidden away from society, her parents never let her go outside. However, she was secretly raised as a earthbending tournament fighter and she has been a winner in all these championships until Aang arrived and asked her to teach him Earthbending.

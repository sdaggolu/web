Katara is a waterbender. She is the daughter of Kya and the chief of the village in the southern watertribe, Hakoda. She is the grandaughter of Kanna. She is from the south pole and she is one of the last waterbenders there. She is Sokka's younger sister and Aang's friend. She helps teach Aang waterbending. She wears a traditional watertribe robe, her mother's necklace, and watertribe hair loopies. Throughout the show, Katara changes from being hopeful to being strong and fierce.

Early Life

Sokka is from the Southern Water Tribe. His father is Chief Hakoda and his mother is Kya. During his childhood, Sokka lost his mother during a fire raid. When this happened, Chief Hakoda left the Southern Water tribe in order to fight in the battle against the Fire Nation during the Hundred Years War. Despite his desire to accompany his father and the other water tribe soldiers in battle, Sokka was not permitted to fight because he was too young. Since all the men were fighting the war and there were no other teenage boys living in the village, Sokka was the oldest male in the South Pole, and thus, the leader of the tribe. Sokka, along with his younger sister Katara, was raised by their paternal grandmother who they affectionately call Gran Gran. Throughout his life, Sokka has been taking care of his village by fishing for food and training kids to become great warriors. He protects his village by using his weapons and fighting skills. His entire life changes when he, and Katara, discover Aang in the iceberg.

Appearance

Sokka has black hair, blue eyes, and brown skin. Sokka wears a traditional water tribal outfit. He wears a dark blue and kimono-like tunic that has split sides over his long-sleeved blue shirt. This outfit is worn over his navy-blue trousers. He also wears brown boots and white and navy-blue arm warmers. He also wears a warrior’s wolf tail where two sides of the head are shaved and hair that is growing on the top is pulled back and tied in a short ponytail. Sokka wears a choker that is made of walrus ivory. He carries a boomerang satchel where he stores his metal boomerang. His boomerang is Sokka’s most essential weapon in fighting alongside his friends. Sokka is 15 years old.

Mission

After Aang got caught up in a storm and was hidden in the iceberg for a hundred years, he was discovered by Sokka and Katara in the South Pole. Sokka’s mission is to travel around the world in order to help and protect Aang as Aang trains to master all four elements. He is a strategist that helps organize the group during the battles that they may face. All these battles will lead up to the war the Sokka will help fight in. Sokka’s leadership and planning skills will help Aang to travel around the world and learn the four elements quickly and efficiently.
